Welcome to Sanrio Wiki

This website is inspired by Sanrio, a multi-billion dollar Japanese company that creates a wide range of designs and characters mostly based on anthropomorphic animals.

Popular Characters

Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty was born in the suburbs of London. She lives with her parents and her twin sister Mimmy who is her best friend.

Kuromi

Kuromi is a tomboy, but behind her tough appearance, she is actually very girly. She enjoys writing in her diary and reading romance novels.

My Melody

My Melody was born in the forest of Mariland. Her favorite hobby is baking cookies with her mother, and her favorite food is almond pound cake.
